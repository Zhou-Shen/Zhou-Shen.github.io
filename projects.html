Hand Posture Recognition

In this project, I compared the performances of five classifiers (Naive Bayes, SVM, KNN, Perceptron, LR) on unlabeled hand postures dataset. The dataset includes 5 types of hand postures from 12 users. The raw data form was preprocessed in three methods - standardization, normalization and PCA. The results show that the classifier SVM with 'rbf' kernal has the best performance, the accuracy is 96.4%.
